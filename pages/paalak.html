Paalak curry

Paalak curry Ingredients

  • 1 bunch spinach
  • 1 chopped tomato
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2 teaspoon spice chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 2 pinches salt
  • 2 chopped onion
  • 1/2 teaspoon chopped garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon powdered turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 2 tablespoon yoghurt (curd)
  • 2 tablespoon refined oil

Steps to make it

Step 1 Make a paste by grinding the onions, tomatoes and garlic. Whip the spinach in the blender and keep aside.

Step 2 Heat oil in a pan, fry the cumin seeds and add the paste prepared earlier. Cover and cook till the gravy is thick, then mix the spice powders and salt and stir well.

Step 3 Now add the spinach puree, reduce the heat and cook for 10 minutes.

Finally, after switching off the flame, add yogurt and mix well.
